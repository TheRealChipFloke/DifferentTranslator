how do i upload the Different Translator — Morse & Binary

Different Translator

Translate between text and "languages" like Morse and Binary (8-bit).

For Morse decode, separate letters with spaces and words with / (or spaces + /). Example (encode): HELLO WORLD → .... . .-.. .-.. --- / .-- --- .-. .-.. -..
Press Translate or use keyboard shortcuts: Ctrl+Enter to translate, Escape to clear.
Examples: Morse for SOS: ... --- ... · Text "A" → Binary: 01000001
